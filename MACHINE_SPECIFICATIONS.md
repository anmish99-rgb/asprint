# Machine Specifications & Information

## 1. UV PRINTING MACHINE

### Overview
UV printing is a digital printing technology that uses ultraviolet light to dry and cure specially formulated inks instantly as they are applied to the substrate. It's often called "direct-to-object" printing because it can print directly on surfaces.

### Technical Specifications
- **Resolution**: 1200 x 1200 DPI (can produce photographic quality)
- **Print Speed**: Up to 50-100 square feet per hour
- **Color Range**: Full CMYK + White + Special colors
- **Ink Type**: UV-curable inks
- **Drying Time**: Instant (UV curing)
- **Substrate Compatibility**: Acrylic, vinyl, rubber, plastic, foam, coated metals
- **Typical Thickness**: Up to 2-3 inches

### Applications at Abhinav Sign & Print
- Direct printing on acrylic sheets for signage
- Vinyl decal printing with precise colors
- Logo printing on various materials
- Custom design printing for customer branding
- High-quality color reproduction for retail displays

### Advantages
- No pre-coating required
- Instant curing - ready to install immediately
- Excellent color vibrancy and accuracy
- Can print on uneven surfaces
- Environmentally friendly (no solvent emission)
- High detail and photographic quality possible

### Maintenance
- Regular cleaning of print heads
- UV lamp replacement after 1000-2000 hours
- Ink cartridge replacement as needed
- Temperature and humidity control

## 2. CNC ROUTER

### Overview
A CNC (Computer Numerical Control) Router is an automated cutting and engraving machine that uses specialized cutting tools to precisely cut and carve materials. It's controlled by computer software for absolute precision.

### Technical Specifications
- **Cutting Area**: Typically 4' x 8' (can vary)
- **Z-Axis Height**: 4-6 inches (for 3D engraving)
- **Spindle Speed**: 5,000 to 18,000 RPM
- **Cutting Speed**: Up to 300 inches per minute
- **Accuracy**: ±0.01 inches
- **Tool Options**: Various cutting bits, V-groove tools, V-notch tools
- **Control Software**: CAD compatible (AutoCAD, CorelDRAW)

### Materials that Can be Cut
- Acrylic (cast and extruded)
- MDF and plywood
- Solid wood
- Soft plastics
- Composite materials
- Leather
- Rubber
- Aluminum (thin sheets)
- Phenolic boards

### Applications at Abhinav Sign & Print

- 3D engraving for logo designs
- Channel letter component cutting
- Pattern and template creation
- Decorative edge effects
- Material prototyping for design verification

### Cutting Examples

- Multi-layer composite signs

- Precision edge beveling
- Contoured shapes with smooth curves

### Advantages
- High precision (±0.01")
- Repeatable accuracy for batch production

## 3. CO2 LASER CUTTING & ENGRAVING MACHINE

### Overview
A CO2 Laser uses a laser beam for precise cutting and engraving. The laser is focused through optics to create a very thin, precise cut that leaves clean edges without any material residue.

### Technical Specifications
- **Laser Type**: CO2 (typically 40-150W)
- **Cutting Area**: 4' x 8' (varies by machine)
- **Wavelength**: 10.6 micrometers
- **Cutting Speed**: Variable based on material (up to 40 inches/second)
- **Resolution**: Up to 1000 DPI for engraving
- **Thickness**: Can cut through up to 1/4"+ materials
- **Cooling**: Water cooling system (chiller required)

### Materials that Can be Cut

✅ Wood (hardwood and softwood)
✅ Plywood
✅ Cardboard
✅ Leather (genuine leather, not synthetic)

✅ Paper and cardstock
✅ Cork
✅ Mylar

❌ PVC (releases toxic fumes)
❌ Polycarbonate (melts)
❌ Fiberglass
❌ Metals (except for engraving with special coatings)

### Applications at Abhinav Sign & Print
- Precision cutting of acrylic for light boxes
- Detailed engraving on acrylic and wood
- Leather engraving for corporate gifts
- Fabric cutting for display pieces
- Wooden sign production
- Multi-color engraving and marking

### Engraving Capabilities
- Photo-quality engraving on acrylic, wood
- Custom color marking (can burn multiple colors)

### Advantages
- Non-contact cutting - no tool wear

- Can cut fine details and intricate patterns

## 4. SPOT WELDING MACHINE

### Overview
Spot welding is an electrical resistance welding process that joins two or more metal sheets together by applying pressure and electrical current. It creates a small "spot" weld at the point of contact.

### Technical Specifications
- **Input Power**: 3-phase, 440V, 50Hz
- **Welding Current**: 1000-5000 Amperes (adjustable)
- **Electrode Force**: 200-2000 Kgs (pneumatic or hydraulic)
- **Cycle Time**: 2-10 cycles per second
- **Electrode Material**: Copper or copper alloy
- **Cooling**: Water-cooled electrodes
- **Working Area**: Varies (typically 2-4 feet between electrodes)

### Technical Process
1. Two metal sheets are placed between copper electrodes
2. Pressure is applied to press sheets together
3. High electrical current passes through for 0.1-0.5 seconds
4. Resistance creates heat (Joule heat) at the interface
5. Metal melts and fuses together
6. Current stops, pressure maintained for cooling

### Applications at Abhinav Sign & Print
- Joining steel plates for letter backing structures
- Attaching steel brackets to signage frames
- Connecting support structures for channel letters
- Welding metal components in LED display frames
- Creating strong joints for outdoor signage
- Joining 304 stainless steel components

### Materials Suitable for Spot Welding
✅ Mild Steel (1-3mm thickness)
✅ Stainless Steel (304, 316 grades)
✅ Galvanized Steel
✅ Aluminum (specialized process)

### Metal Thickness Capability
- Thin sheets: 0.5-1mm
- Regular sheets: 1-3mm
- Thick plates: 3-6mm (requires higher current)
- Maximum: Generally limited to 6mm

### Weld Strength
- Tensile strength: 200-400 N/mm² (depending on material)
- In well-executed spot welds, can achieve 50% of base material strength
- Suitable for structural applications in signage

### Advantages
- Fast production (many welds per minute)
- Clean appearance (no flux residue)
- No filler material required
- Minimal distortion or warping
- Repeatability - consistent quality
- Suitable for automation
- No grinding or finishing needed

### Quality Factors
- Electrode pressure consistency
- Correct passage time
- Current stability
- Electrode condition and cleanliness
- Material cleanliness and surface condition

### Maintenance Required
- Regular electrode cleaning/dressing
- Water flow verification for cooling
- Pressure adjustment verification
- Current calibration checks

## 5. CHANNEL & STEEL BENDING MACHINE

### Overview
A channel and steel bending machine is specialized equipment used to bend metal channels, angles, and profiles into specific shapes and radii. It's essential for creating custom channel letters and structural components.

### Technical Specifications
- **Bending Capacity**: Up to 100x100mm channels (varies by machine)
- **Bending Radii**: From extremely tight (200mm) to large curves
- **Maximum Material Thickness**: 6mm steel or equivalent
- **Bending Angles**: 0-180 degrees (full range)
- **Precision**: ±1-2 degrees
- **Motor Power**: 5-15 HP
- **Speed**: 5-50 bends per hour

### Bendable Materials
- Mild steel channels (UPN profiles)
- Stainless steel channels (304, 316)
- Aluminum channels (for lighter applications)
- Steel angles
- Steel flats (up to specific width)
- Brass and copper channels

### Bending Capabilities
1. **Straight line bending** - Simple 180-degree bends
2. **Single curve bends** - Even radius throughout
3. **Spiral bending** - Gradually changing radius
4. **3D bending** - Complex multi-plane bending
5. **Partial bends** - Specific sections of profile

### Channel Letter Production Process
The machine allows creation of:
- Letter outlines from channels or flats
- 3D channel letter shapes
- Custom enclosures
- Support brackets
- Mounting structures

### Applications at Abhinav Sign & Print
- Creating channel letter "blanks"
- Bending aluminum channel edges for LED integration
- Fabricating custom frames for LED displays
- Creating support structures for large signs
- Producing structural components for signage systems
- Manufacturing custom metal components per client specifications

### Typical Channel Sizes Used
- 20x20mm to 100x100mm channels
- Various flange widths and thicknesses
- Different material grades from mild to stainless steel

### Bend Radius Considerations
- **Minimum bend radius (R)**: Generally R = 1-1.5x material thickness
- **Example**: 3mm material minimum internal radius = 3-4.5mm
- Smaller radii can cause cracking or fracture

### Advantages
- Precision bending repeatable
- Various radius capabilities
- Minimal material waste
- Quick setup for production runs
- Can achieve complex 3D shapes
- Professional quality results
- Suitable for both prototyping and batch production

### Quality Factors
- Accuracy maintained throughout bends
- No crack formation
- Minimal springback (elastic recovery)
- Each bend executed to specification
- Proper alignment and tool selection

### Typical Projects
- Custom channel letters (any size)
- 3D letter arrays
- Frame structures for LED boards
- Decorative enclosures
- Signage support structures
- Bracket fabrication

## 6. LED LIGHT BOARDS & DISPLAYS

### Overview
LED Light Boards are modern signage solutions using Light Emitting Diodes for energy-efficient, bright, and attention-grabbing displays. They're ideal for retail, corporate, and promotional signage.

### Types of LED Solutions

#### A. LED Strip Integration
- **Specifications**: 
  - SMD LEDs (Surface Mount Device)
  - RGB or Single Color (White, Red, Blue, Green)
  - 12V or 24V DC power
  - 60-120 LEDs per meter
  - Power consumption: 5-15W per meter
  
- **Applications**:
  - Backlighting for channel letters
  - Edge lighting for acrylic panels
  - Outline lighting for sign perimeters
  - Accent lighting for architectural features

#### B. Full-Color LED Displays
- **Specifications**:
  - Pixel pitch: 4-16mm (outdoor/indoor)
  - Resolution: Variable (P4 means 4mm pixel pitch)
  - Brightness: 500-2000 nits (indoor to outdoor)
  - Colors: 16 million colors
  - Control: Wi-Fi or Ethernet connectivity
  - Viewing angle: 110-140 degrees

- **Applications**:
  - Storefront advertisement displays
  - Real-time information boards
  - Dynamic content displays
  - Video-capable signage

#### C. Programmable LED Boards
- **Features**:
  - Microcontroller-based systems
  - Customizable animations and patterns
  - Time-based scheduling
  - Multi-zone control
  - SMS or app-based content updates
  
- **Applications**:
  - Business name displays
  - Message boards for retailers
  - Promotional displays
  - Information boards

### Technical Specifications (Typical)
- **Operating Temperature**: -10°C to +50°C
- **Power Supply**: 12V DC, 24V DC, or AC (depending on type)
- **Lifespan**: 50,000-100,000 hours (5-11 years typical)
- **Efficacy**: 80-100 lumens per watt
- **Waterproof Rating**: IP65-IP68 for outdoor
- **Material**: Aluminum frames with diffuser panels

### Installation Process
1. Design layout and mounting structure
2. Install aluminum channel housing
3. Mount and connect LED strips
4. Integrate power supply (transformer for 12/24V DC)
5. Connect control system (if applicable)
6. Install diffuser or cover panels
7. Test all connections and brightness

### Energy Efficiency
- **LED vs Traditional Neon**: Uses 50-70% less energy
- **LED vs Incandescent**: Uses 80-90% less energy
- **Operating cost**: Minimal compared to other lighting options
- **Heat generation**: Minimal (minimal cooling required)

### Color Options Available
- Pure White (5000-6500K daylight)
- Warm White (2700-3000K)
- Red (590-630nm)
- Green (510-550nm)
- Blue (450-500nm)
- RGB (changeable, programmable)
- Amber (yellow-orange tones)

### Brightness Levels
Different applications require different brightness:
- **Indoor daytime**: 200-500 nits
- **Indoor nighttime**: 50-200 nits
- **Outdoor daytime**: 1000-2000 nits
- **Outdoor nighttime**: 200-800 nits

### Advantages
- High brightness and visibility
- Long operational lifespan
- Low energy consumption
- Instant on/off (no warm-up time)
- Variety of colors available
- Programmable and customizable
- Maintenance-free operation
- Environmentally friendly
- Modern appearance
- Excellent for branding

### Applications in Signage

2. **Outline lighting** - Creates visual boundaries

4. **Menu boards** - For restaurants/bars
